# Privacy Policy for 28 Weeks in Hell

**Effective date:** 29 March 2026

28 Weeks in Hell is a local-first fitness game system designed to help users track training, progression, reminders, and related in-app features.

## 1. Data Collection
28 Weeks in Hell does not collect, store, or share personal data on external servers.

All progress and app-related data are stored locally on the user's device.

## 2. Notifications
The app may use local notifications and reminders created on the user's device to support training schedules and app reminders.

These notifications are generated locally and are not used to collect personal data.

## 3. External Links
The app may contain external links to social media pages or donation pages. When a user taps one of these links, they are redirected to an external website or app.

28 Weeks in Hell is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third-party websites or services.

## 4. Data Storage
User progress, settings, and related app data are stored locally on the device. This data is not transmitted to the developer.

## 5. Accounts
28 Weeks in Hell does not require account creation or login.

## 6. Data Deletion
Because the app does not store user data on external servers, users can delete locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage on their device.

## 7. Children
28 Weeks in Hell is not directed at children.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
